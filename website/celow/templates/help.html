Objective

  1. The objective of the game is to roll a winning hand or the highest point.

Rules

  1. Place any number you wish in the wager field to place a bet.
  2. {% block rollmethod %}{% endblock %}
  3. After you roll you will see the result of your roll in the dice and by a banner at the top of window
    • If you win the money that you wagered will be added to your bank on the left of the screen.
    • If you lose then the money that you wagered will be subtracted from your bank on the left of the screen.
    • Your wager field will go to 0 after each roll so, if you want to continue to wager the you must enter a new number each time.
  4. Winning Hands
    • A winning hand is when you roll either a 4,5,6 or 3 dice of the same number ex.: 1,1,1 or 6,6,6; or you roll any 2 dice with the same number and the last is a 6. ex.: 2,2,6 or 2,6,2.
    • If you receive a winning hand then you will see a banner at the top of the screen that says "You Win !!!!!!!" and the money that you wagered will be added to your bank on the left of the screen.
  5. Point
    • It is when you roll the dice and receive 2 dice that have the same number, your point is the dice with the different number. ex.: 4,4,5; Your point would be 5.
    • If you receive a point then you will see a banner at the top of the screen that says "Point, Please Roll for the Computer !!!!!!" and you will see a your point number in the lower left hand side of the screen.
    • You must then roll for the computer at this time to see if it can beat, push or lose to your point
      • A point value is beaten if a higher point value is rolled. ex.: 4,4,5 would beat 3,3,2
      • A point value is a push if an equal point value is rolled. ex.: 4,4,5 would push 3,3,5
      • A point is lost if a lower point value is rolled. ex.: 4,4,2 would lose to 3,3,5
    • If you point was a winner to the computers then you will see a banner at the top of the screen that says "You Win !!!!!!!" and the money that you wagered will be added to your bank on the left of the screen.
    • If you point was a loser you will see a banner at the top of the screen that says "You Lose !!!!!!!" and the money that you wagered will be subtracted to your bank on the left of the screen.
    • If you point pushed then you will see a banner at the top of the screen that says "Computer Pushes, Your Turn" and bank does not change.
  6. Losing hands
    • A losing hand is when you roll either a 1,2,3 or any 2 dice with the same number and the last is a 1. ex.: 2,2,1 or 2,1,2.
    • If you receive a winning hand then you will see a banner at the top of the screen that says "You Lose !!!!!!!" and the money that you wagered will be subtracted to your bank on the left of the screen.
  7. To play again just repeat the process
